Spaghetties(spags) recipe:

-Boil the spags
-Place in called water when they're ready
-Prepare and fry tomatoes, onions and any others(such as green paper), adding 
salt and other condiments you'd like
-Sieve the spags out of the water and place in the saucepan with the fried toma
toes etc still on the stove
-Mix to get even coloring and taste
